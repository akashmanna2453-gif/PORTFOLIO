# About Me

I'm Akash, a passionate cybersecurity professional dedicated to making the digital world safer. With expertise in ethical hacking and penetration testing, I help organizations identify and fix security vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them.

My journey began with a curiosity about how systems work, which evolved into a mission to understand how they break—and how to prevent it. I've successfully identified critical vulnerabilities in various platforms and have been acknowledged by several tech companies for responsible disclosure.

When I'm not hunting bugs or writing exploits, I'm developing security tools and contributing to open-source security projects. I believe in the power of knowledge sharing and regularly conduct workshops on cybersecurity awareness.
